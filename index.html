MicroJournal - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

MicroJournal ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal micro-journaling application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. MicroJournal works entirely on your device. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any contact information
  • We do not access your contacts, files, or location
  • We do not read or transmit your journal entries
  • Your journal entries, photos, and mood data are stored ONLY on your device

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Journal entries (text, mood, date)
  • Photos you attach to entries
  • App settings (theme, reminder, lock preferences)
  • Usage statistics (streaks, entry counts)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

For more information: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Google > Ads.

Photos

When you attach a photo to a journal entry, the photo is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, transmit, or access your photos in any way.

App Lock (PIN / Biometric)

If you enable app lock, your PIN is stored locally on your device. Biometric data (fingerprint) is handled entirely by your device's operating system — we never access or store biometric data.

Children's Privacy

MicroJournal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Permissions

  • Internet: Required for displaying advertisements
  • Notifications: For daily writing reminders
  • Biometric: For optional app lock feature
  • Photo access: Only when you choose to attach a photo

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
